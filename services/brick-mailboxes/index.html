Brick Mailboxes and Letterboxes

A brick mailbox or letterbox is a practical and attractive addition to any front fence or driveway entrance. Built from matching bricks or blocks, a brick mailbox creates a cohesive look with your fence and adds a premium touch to your property's street frontage.

In Brisbane, brick mailboxes are commonly built as standalone piers at the driveway entrance, or integrated into the front fence near the gate. They can be designed with a single mail slot, a rear-access door, or a parcel compartment depending on your needs.

Brick Mailbox Types

  • Standalone brick mailbox – a single brick pier at the front boundary with a mail slot.
  • Integrated brick mailbox – built into the front fence wall, matching the fence brickwork.
  • Brick mailbox with parcel box – larger design with a locked compartment for parcels.
  • Block mailbox – built from concrete blocks and rendered or painted for a modern look.
  • Custom letterbox – designed with feature brickwork, caps, and lighting.

Brick Mailbox Cost Brisbane

Brick mailbox costs in Brisbane typically range from $800 to $2,500 depending on the size, materials, and finish. A simple brick mailbox on a standard 230mm pier is at the lower end, while a large custom mailbox with parcel compartment, lighting, and feature caps is at the higher end.

The mailbox insert itself is an additional cost, with basic stainless steel inserts starting around $80 and premium parcel boxes costing $300 or more.

Design and Compliance

Brick mailboxes should be built on a concrete footing and bonded securely to the fence or freestanding pier. They should be positioned within easy reach of the footpath for postal workers and positioned to avoid blocking driveways or footpaths.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a brick mailbox cost?

A brick mailbox in Brisbane costs $800 to $2,500 depending on size, materials, and whether it includes a parcel box or custom features.

Can a mailbox be built into a brick fence?

Yes, brick mailboxes are often built into the front fence for a seamless look and to save space.

Do brick mailboxes need a footing?

Yes, all brick mailboxes require a concrete footing to prevent movement and ensure stability.

What size is a standard brick mailbox pier?

A standard brick mailbox pier is usually 230mm x 230mm or 350mm x 350mm, depending on the mailbox insert size.
