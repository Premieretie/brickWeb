Brick Wall Applications

Brick walls are one of the most versatile masonry structures for residential and commercial properties in Brisbane. They can be used for garden walls, feature walls, privacy screens, courtyard enclosures, and boundary walls. A properly built brick wall adds structure, style, and long-term value to any property.

Brick walls differ from brick fences mainly in purpose and location. A fence is typically a boundary structure, while a wall can be a freestanding feature, a retaining structure, or part of a landscape design.

Brick Wall Types

  • Garden brick wall – low decorative wall for garden beds, planters, or terracing.
  • Feature brick wall – decorative wall with patterned brickwork, contrasting mortar, or mixed colours.
  • Privacy brick wall – taller wall built to block sightlines between properties.
  • Retaining brick wall – wall designed to hold back soil, often requiring engineering.
  • Plinth wall – short brick base with a different fence material on top.

Brick Wall Cost Brisbane

Brick wall costs in Brisbane range from $280 to $520 per square metre depending on the wall thickness, brick type, finish, and height. Single face walls are at the lower end, while double face, rendered, or feature walls are at the higher end.

Footings, excavation, drainage, and council approval can add to the cost. For retaining walls, engineering design and steel reinforcement are additional expenses.

Engineering and Regulations

Brick walls over 1.8 metres or walls retaining soil may require building approval and engineering certification. A licensed builder or structural engineer should assess any wall that is load-bearing or retaining.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a brick wall cost per square metre?

Brick walls in Brisbane cost $280 to $520 per square metre depending on thickness, brick type, and finish.

Do I need a permit for a brick wall?

Walls over 1.8 metres or retaining walls over 1.0 metre usually require approval in Brisbane. Always check with your local council.

Can a brick wall be a retaining wall?

Yes, but retaining brick walls must be engineered and reinforced to handle soil pressure and drainage.

How long does a brick wall last?

A well-built brick wall with proper footings can last 50 years or more with minimal maintenance.
