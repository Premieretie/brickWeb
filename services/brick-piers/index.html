Brick Piers and Columns

Brick piers, also known as brick columns or pillars, are vertical masonry supports used in fences, walls, and gates. They add strength, structure, and visual appeal to a fence line. Piers are typically placed at fence corners, gate openings, and at regular intervals along long boundary walls.

In Brisbane, brick piers are commonly built in standard sizes of 230mm, 350mm, or 460mm square. Larger piers are often used for driveway gates and double gates where additional load bearing is required.

Brick Pier Types

  • Standard brick pier – 230mm or 350mm square, used at regular intervals along a fence.
  • Gate pier – larger pier built to support a gate and latch hardware.
  • Feature pier – decorative pier with contrasting brick, capping, or recessed panels.
  • Block pier – concrete block pier, often core-filled and rendered.
  • Plinth – short raised brick base under the fence, adding height and a finished look.

Brick Pier Cost Brisbane

Brick pier costs in Brisbane typically range from $400 to $1,200 per pier depending on size, height, footing, and finish. Standard 230mm piers are the most economical, while larger gate piers with feature caps cost more.

The pier cost includes the concrete footing, brickwork, mortar, and capping. If the pier is supporting a gate, additional steel reinforcement may be required.

When Do You Need Piers?

Most brick and block fences benefit from piers at regular intervals. Engineering and council guidelines often specify maximum spacing between piers, especially for taller walls or walls in wind-prone areas. Gate openings always require reinforced piers on both sides.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do brick piers cost?

Brick piers in Brisbane cost $400 to $1,200 each depending on size, height, and footing requirements.

How far apart should brick piers be?

Pier spacing depends on wall height and engineering, but 3 to 6 metres is common for standard fences.

What size pier do I need for a gate?

Gate piers are typically 350mm or 460mm square and must be reinforced to handle gate weight and wind loads.

Do brick piers need footings?

Yes, every brick pier requires a concrete footing to prevent settling and movement.
