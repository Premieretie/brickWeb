What Is a Retaining Wall?

A retaining wall is a structure built to hold back soil and create level changes in a landscape. Retaining walls are essential for sloped blocks, tiered gardens, driveway cuttings, and properties where the ground level differs between neighbouring boundaries.

In Brisbane, retaining walls are commonly built from concrete blocks, bricks, or reinforced concrete. Block retaining walls are the most popular choice due to their strength, cost-effectiveness, and ability to handle large soil loads.

Retaining Wall Types

  • Block retaining wall – concrete masonry blocks core-filled with concrete and steel.
  • Brick retaining wall – clay brick wall with reinforced concrete core, often used for aesthetic boundaries.
  • Gravity retaining wall – relies on mass and weight to resist soil pressure.
  • Cantilever retaining wall – reinforced concrete footing and stem wall, designed by an engineer.
  • Timber or sleeper retaining wall – cheaper but shorter lifespan, often used for garden beds.

Retaining Wall Cost Brisbane

Retaining wall costs in Brisbane depend heavily on height, length, soil type, drainage, and engineering requirements. As a general guide:

  • Block retaining walls: $250 to $450 per square metre
  • Brick retaining walls: $350 to $600 per square metre
  • Engineering and certification: $1,500 to $5,000+ depending on complexity

Walls over 1.0 metre in height generally require engineering design and council approval. Our calculator helps estimate the masonry component of the project. For detailed engineering, speak with a licensed builder or structural engineer.

Engineering and Compliance

Retaining walls must be designed to handle lateral earth pressure, water pressure, and surcharge loads. In Queensland, walls over 1.0 metre high typically require a structural engineer's certification. Drainage is critical to prevent water buildup behind the wall, which can cause failure.

All retaining walls should comply with the Queensland Building and Construction Commission (QBCC) and local council requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a retaining wall cost in Brisbane?

Block retaining walls cost $250 to $450 per square metre, and brick retaining walls cost $350 to $600 per square metre. Engineering is additional for walls over 1.0 metre.

Do retaining walls need council approval?

Retaining walls over 1.0 metre generally require engineering certification and possibly council approval in Brisbane.

Can a brick fence be a retaining wall?

Yes, but it must be engineered as a retaining structure with proper footings, reinforcement, and drainage.

How long do retaining walls last?

Concrete block retaining walls can last 40 to 60 years. Brick retaining walls can last 50+ years if properly designed and drained.
