Before building a brick fence or wall in Moreton Bay, check with Moreton Bay Regional Council regarding height limits, setback requirements, and retaining wall approvals. Front fences are generally limited to 1.8 metres, and boundary walls to 2.0 metres. Retaining walls over 1.0 metre typically require engineering certification.
