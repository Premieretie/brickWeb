How to Choose a Bricklayer in Brisbane

A practical guide to finding the right bricklayer for your fence, wall, or retaining wall.

Choosing the right bricklayer is one of the most important decisions for your fence or wall project. A skilled bricklayer will deliver a straight, level, and durable wall that enhances your property. Here is what to look for when hiring a bricklayer in Brisbane.

1. Check QBCC Licence and Insurance

In Queensland, most building work over $3,300 must be carried out by a QBCC-licensed contractor. Ask for the bricklayer's licence number and verify it on the QBCC website. Also confirm they have public liability insurance.

2. Review Past Work

Ask to see photos of previous fences, walls, or retaining walls. Look for clean mortar joints, consistent courses, straight lines, and quality finishing. A reputable bricklayer will be happy to share examples.

3. Get a Detailed Written Quote

A proper quote should include the scope of work, materials, labour, footings, piers, cleanup, and estimated timeframe. Avoid quotes that are vague or verbal only. Use our calculator to benchmark your price.

4. Ask About Experience With Your Project Type

Not all bricklayers specialise in fences. Some focus on houses, while others are experienced in boundary walls, retaining walls, and feature work. Choose someone with relevant experience.

5. Check Reviews and References

Read online reviews and ask for references from recent clients. A reliable bricklayer will have a history of completing projects on time and to a high standard.

6. Understand the Timeline

Ask how long the project will take and what factors could cause delays. Weather, material availability, and site access can all affect the schedule.

Conclusion

Taking time to choose the right bricklayer can save you money and stress in the long run. A well-built brick fence or wall will last decades and add value to your home.
