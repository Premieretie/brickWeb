How Much Does a Brick Mailbox Cost?

Brisbane pricing guide for brick mailboxes and letterboxes.

A brick mailbox is a practical and attractive addition to any front fence or driveway. It adds a polished look to your property and can be built to match your fence. In this guide, we explain the typical costs for brick mailboxes in Brisbane and what affects the price.

Brick Mailbox Cost Range

Brick mailboxes in Brisbane typically cost between $800 and $2,500. The price depends on the size, design, materials, and whether the mailbox is standalone or integrated into the fence.

Types of Brick Mailboxes

  • Standard brick mailbox: $800 – $1,400. A simple brick pier with a standard letterbox insert.
  • Integrated brick mailbox: $1,200 – $2,000. Built into the front fence for a seamless look.
  • Parcel brick mailbox: $1,800 – $2,500+. Larger design with locked parcel compartment.
  • Block mailbox: $900 – $1,800. Concrete block construction, often rendered or painted.

What Affects the Cost?

The main factors affecting brick mailbox cost are pier size, footing depth, brick type, mailbox insert quality, and any custom features such as lighting or decorative caps. A matching brick mailbox with your front fence will usually cost more than a standalone pier because it requires careful alignment with the fence brickwork.
