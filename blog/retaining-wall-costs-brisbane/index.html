Retaining Wall Costs Brisbane 2026

A complete price guide for retaining walls in Brisbane and South East Queensland.

Retaining walls are essential for sloping blocks, tiered gardens, and properties where level changes need to be managed. In Brisbane, retaining walls are commonly built from concrete blocks, bricks, or reinforced concrete. The cost depends on height, length, materials, and engineering requirements.

Retaining Wall Costs Per Square Metre

  • Block retaining wall: $250 – $450 per m²
  • Brick retaining wall: $350 – $600 per m²
  • Reinforced concrete retaining wall: $400 – $800 per m²

Engineering and Certification Costs

Retaining walls over 1.0 metre in height generally require a structural engineer's design and council approval in Brisbane. Engineering fees typically range from $1,500 to $5,000 depending on the complexity of the wall and site conditions.

What Affects Retaining Wall Costs?

Key factors include wall height, soil type, drainage requirements, access, surcharge loads, and whether the wall is built on a boundary. Taller walls and walls retaining large soil volumes cost significantly more.

Drainage Is Critical

Without proper drainage, water pressure behind the wall can cause cracking, bulging, or complete failure. All retaining walls should include drainage provisions such as ag pipes and gravel backfill.
