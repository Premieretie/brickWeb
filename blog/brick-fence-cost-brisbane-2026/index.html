Brick Fence Cost Brisbane 2026

A complete price guide for brick and block fences in Brisbane and South East Queensland.

Planning a brick fence in Brisbane in 2026? Understanding current pricing is essential for budgeting and choosing the right materials. This guide covers the typical costs for single face, double face, rendered, and block fences across Brisbane, Ipswich, Logan, Redlands, Moreton Bay, Gold Coast, and Sunshine Coast.

Brick Fence Prices Per Square Metre

Brick fence costs are usually calculated per square metre of wall area. The rate depends on the brick type, wall thickness, finish, and site conditions.

  • Single face brick fence: $280 – $420 per m²
  • Double face brick fence: $380 – $520 per m²
  • Rendered brick fence: $430 – $640 per m²
  • Smooth face block fence: $180 – $280 per m²
  • Split face block fence: $220 – $320 per m²

Brick Fence Prices Per Linear Metre

For a 1.8 metre high fence, typical costs per metre are:

  • Single face brick: $500 – $750 per linear metre
  • Double face brick: $680 – $940 per linear metre
  • Block fence: $320 – $580 per linear metre

What Affects the Cost?

Several factors influence the final price of a brick fence:

  • Height: Taller fences require more materials, labour, and stronger footings.
  • Length: Longer fences cost more overall but may have a lower per-metre rate.
  • Material: Premium face bricks and rendered finishes cost more than standard bricks or blocks.
  • Piers: Decorative or reinforced piers add cost but improve strength and appearance.
  • Footings: Soil type, slope, and access affect footing costs.
  • Access: Difficult sites may require additional labour or equipment.
  • Extras: Gates, letterboxes, rendering, painting, and lighting increase the total.

Conclusion

Brick fence costs in Brisbane in 2026 remain competitive compared to other major Australian cities. By choosing the right material and design, you can achieve a durable, attractive fence that fits your budget.
